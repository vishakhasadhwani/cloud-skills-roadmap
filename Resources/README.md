## Top 5 Certifications with Tools

### 1. Certified Kubernetes Administrator (CKA)

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Provider** | CNCF / Linux Foundation |
| **Format** | Performance-based, 2 hours |
| **Validity** | 2 years |

**Tools to Master:**

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| `kubectl` | Cluster management & debugging |
| `kubeadm` | Cluster bootstrapping |
| `etcd` | Backup & restore |
| `crictl` | Container runtime debugging |
| `systemctl` | Node service management |

**Practice Resources:**

- [killer.sh](https://killer.sh) - CKA simulator (included with exam)
- [kodekloud.com](https://kodekloud.com) - Hands-on labs
- [github.com/walidshaari/Kubernetes-Certified-Administrator](https://github.com/walidshaari/Kubernetes-Certified-Administrator)

## Notes on Using This Resource

- **Free vs paid:** Most YouTube content is free; Udemy courses often go on sale for **$9.99–$14.99**.
- **Hands-on practice:** Always supplement video learning with actual implementation.
- **Projects:** Focus on the **3 main projects** (RAG, Monitoring, CI/CD) for your portfolio.
- **GitHub repos:** Clone and study example repos, then build your own versions.
- **Community:** Join Discord/Slack for real-time help.
- **Interview prep:** Use months **11–12** for behavioral and technical interview resources.
- **Certification:** Consider **CKA** (Kubernetes) or **Terraform Associate** after fundamentals.

This roadmap is designed to take you from **beginner to job-ready in 12 months** with a mix of free and paid resources, focusing on practical skills and portfolio projects.
